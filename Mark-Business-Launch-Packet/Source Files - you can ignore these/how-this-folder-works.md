# Read Me First

Welcome, Mark. This is your whole business in one folder. You do not need to be a computer person to use it. Just open the items in order, one at a time, and read.

Everything is numbered. Start at 1 and work your way down. That is the whole trick.

## Read them in this order

**1. Read Me First.** This page. You are reading it now. It tells you what everything is and the order to go in.

**2. The Vision.** A short, good looking slide show that paints the whole picture on a few clean screens. Start here so you can see where this is all going before you read the details. About ten minutes.

**3. Start Here, The Full Playbook.** This is the main one and the most important. It walks you through everything in plain words. What the business is, why you are perfect for it, what you sell, what the money looks like, how to set up the tool and make your first document, exactly how to pitch your group, and how to do all of this around your health. Read it all the way through, one page at a time.

**4. Your First 30 Days.** Your map for the first month. Week by week, and day by day for the first week, with rest days built right in. After you read the playbook, this tells you exactly what to do each day.

**5. Tools and Examples.** A folder of things you use while you work, not things you read front to back. Open it when the playbook tells you to. Inside you will find:
- Copy and Paste Prompts. Ready made things to paste into the tool so you never face a blank screen.
- Two finished Sample documents, a server training manual and an opening checklist. Read them to see what good work looks like, and use them as proof pieces when you pitch.
- Simple Agreement and Scope. A friendly one page agreement to use when someone says yes.

**6. Research Notes.** The thinking and the proof behind the plan. You do not need this to get started. It is here so you can see that every number and every claim stands on real, public research. Read it only if you want the receipts.

## The short version

If you only do one thing today, open number 2, The Vision, then read number 3, Start Here. Everything else is here when you need it.

## One last note

There is also a folder called Source Files that you can completely ignore. It just holds the plain text and editing versions of these same documents. You never need to open it. Everything you need to read is in the numbered items above.

Take it one step at a time, Mark. You have got this.
