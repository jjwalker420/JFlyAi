# How This Folder Works

Welcome, Mark. This is the whole business in one folder. Read this short page first. It tells you what every file is and the order to open them. Then follow the order and you cannot get lost.

## Read these in this order

**1. The Vision deck.** Open the file `vision/The-Vision.pdf`.
This is the picture of the whole idea on a handful of clean screens. Start here so you can see where everything is going before you read the details. There is also a PowerPoint version, `vision/The-Vision.pptx`, if you ever want to present it or edit it. About ten minutes to read.

**2. START-HERE.md.** This is the main packet and the most important file.
It walks you through everything in plain words. What the business is, why you are the right person, what you sell, what the money looks like, how to set up and make your first document, exactly how to pitch your group, and how to do this around your health. Read it all the way through, one page at a time. Everything else supports this file.

**3. first-30-days/First-30-Days.md.** Your map for the first month.
Week by week, and day by day for the first week, with rest days built in. Once you have read START-HERE.md, this tells you exactly what to do each day.

**4. copy-paste-prompts.md.** Your shortcut so you never face a blank screen.
A ready made prompt for every kind of document you sell. Paste one in, fill in a couple of blanks, and start talking. Keep this open whenever you are building a document.

**5. examples/.** Two finished, polished sample documents.
- `Server-Training-Manual-TapAndBurger.md` is a complete server training manual.
- `Opening-Checklist-FOH-BOH-TapAndBurger.md` is a complete front and back of house opening checklist.

Both are built for a burger and beer style spot, like the ones your group runs. Read them to see what finished work looks like. Use them as the proof pieces you show when you pitch, or rebuild them in your own voice as your first practice documents.

**6. research/research-notes.md.** The thinking behind the plan.
You do not need this to get started. It is here so you can see that every number and every claim in this packet stands on real, public research about you, about The Culinary Creative Group, about how big groups standardize, and about what consultants really charge. Read it only if you want the receipts.

## The short version

If you only do one thing today, open `vision/The-Vision.pdf`, then read `START-HERE.md`. Everything else is here when you need it.

## A note on the files

The documents ending in `.md` are plain text files. You can open them in any text reader, in Notes, or in any word processor. The deck is a `.pdf` you can open on any device, with a `.pptx` PowerPoint version alongside it.

Take it one step at a time, Mark. You have got this.
